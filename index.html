WHAT WE DO

At EnlightYou, the services we provide are a product of our team’s collaborative efforts.

Private and Group Tutoring

Our tutors will teach any subject of your choosing and will adapt their teaching style to provide you the best educational experience.

Examination and Board Reviews

Whether it be for school examinations or board preparation, our team will prepare and host lecture-style review per your class’s request.

Personalized Mentorship

Our mentors will work one-on-one with clients to create an optimized step-by-step plan that enables students to realize their goals.

Research Opportunities

Working alongside our fellow medical and educational colleagues, we provide avenues and opportunities for our clients to partake in research of their interest.

Career Development

Whether it be applying for college or residency, our team offers personal statement editing, interview preparation, and CV/resume editing services to help you stand out from the crowd.

Community Service Opportunities

We strive to give back to the community, both locally and globally. Every month, we will donate a percentage of our proceeds to charity. Moreover, we are currently in the process of creating volunteering opportunities across to globe. We’d love to get both EnlightYou team members and clients to collaborate on this mission of promoting humanity!

01.

CONSULTATION

The EnlightYou team strives to provides personalized guidance specifically tailored for each client. During the initial consult with one of our educational directors, we take the time to appreciate our client’s goals, definition of success, and personality to tailor our services to their personal needs.

02.

CONNECTION

  After developing a clear understanding of our clients and their ambitions, we connect them with a member of the EnlightYou team. We specifically match together an EnlightYou mentor whose teaching style complements the learnings-style of our clients.

03.

COLLABORATION

The EnlightYou mentor provides personalized mentorship and instruction on any of the mentee’s topics of interest. Additionally, we strive the ensure that the EnlightYou mentor-mentee relationship is constantly maturing. Once a week, one of our directors will reach out to the client to obtain feedback so our team can address concerns and assure we are exceeding the expectations of our clients.
